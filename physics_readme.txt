Gogot has 4 node that deal with physics in (2d) 
Area2D, staticBody2D, CharacterBody2D, RigidBody2d

Area2D is just an area(but it's good for detecting collisions)
staticbody2d has collisions but cannot move
characterbody2d has collisions and can move(via code)
RigidBody2D has collisions and can move(via physics)

all 4 nodes need a collision shape
